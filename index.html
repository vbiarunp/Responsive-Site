About Our Organization

Established on 25th October,2013 by the group of students,Agarthulir aims to provide imparty service to the under-privileged and deserving human beings. We strive to bridge the gap between these deserving people by overcoming the barriers in acquiring it. With the firm belief that helping the people at any cost will not only eliminate the social evils but also aid in the socio-economic upbringing of the society, we work towards providing appropriate opportunities to the rural and deserving people who do not have access for the quality of life. The all time moto to our mind is "Greatest service is to anybody to nobody"
